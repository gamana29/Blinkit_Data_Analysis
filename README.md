## 📘 **Project Overview**

This project analyzes **Blinkit grocery sales and outlet data** to uncover actionable business insights.  
It covers sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, ratings analysis, and outlet performance metrics.

The entire pipeline is designed so you can perform **sales and outlet analysis without direct access to Blinkit's internal systems**.

---

## 🌐 **Flow of the Entire Project**

### **1️⃣ Data Collection & Cleaning**  
- Import sales, product, and outlet datasets  
- Handle missing values, duplicates, and inconsistent entries  
- Normalize and format data for analysis  

### **2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**  
- Analyze sales trends over time  
- Identify top-selling products and categories  
- Examine customer ratings and review patterns  
- Detect seasonal or regional demand fluctuations  

### **3️⃣ SQL Queries for Business Insights**  
- Aggregate sales by outlet, category, and date  
- Identify top-performing outlets and low-performing regions  
- Calculate revenue, profit margins, and growth rates  

### **4️⃣ Visualization & Reporting**  
- Generate charts in Python and Power BI  
- Heatmaps for outlet performance  
- Trend lines for sales over months  
- Customer rating distribution plots  

### **5️⃣ Insights & Recommendations**  
- Highlight peak sales periods  
- Recommend inventory adjustments  
- Suggest marketing strategies for low-performing products  
- Detect opportunities for new product launches
